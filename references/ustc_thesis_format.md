# USTC Thesis Word Format

Source template analyzed:

- `F:\硕士\毕业相关\中国科技大学博士学位论文撰写模板（新）.doc`
- Converted asset for reuse: `assets/ustc_phd_thesis_template.docx`

Use this reference whenever the user asks for a Chinese thesis, dissertation, graduation thesis, USTC/中国科学技术大学 formatting, or wants generated Word files to follow the school thesis style.

## Base Page Setup

- Paper: A4, 21.0 cm × 29.7 cm.
- Margins: top 2.54 cm, bottom 2.54 cm, left 3.17 cm, right 3.17 cm.
- Gutter: 0 cm, left.
- Header distance: 2.0 cm for thesis body/front matter from abstract onward.
- Footer distance: 1.75 cm.
- Page numbering:
  - Cover and originality/authorization statement: no page number.
  - Abstract through table of contents: Roman numerals `Ⅰ, Ⅱ, Ⅲ...`.
  - Main body from `第1章  引言`: Arabic numerals `1, 2, 3...`.
  - Page number centered in footer, Times New Roman 10.5 pt, single line spacing, no decorative lines.
- Header:
  - From `摘要` through the end, every page has a centered header.
  - Header content is the current chapter-level title.
  - Chinese font `宋体`, English font `Times New Roman`, 10.5 pt, single line spacing.
  - Header has a horizontal line as shown in the template.

## Required Document Order

1. Chinese cover.
2. English cover.
3. Originality statement and authorization statement.
4. Innovation statement, if required.
5. Chinese abstract and keywords.
6. English abstract and keywords.
7. Table of contents.
8. List of figures and tables, when needed.
9. Main body, starting on an odd page with `第1章  引言` or `第1章  绪论`.
10. References.
11. Appendices, if any.
12. Acknowledgements.
13. Research achievements during study.

If generated output is only a chapter/section rather than a full thesis, still apply the body text, heading, reference, table, figure, margin, and QA rules.

## Body Text

- Chinese font: `宋体`.
- English letters and Arabic numerals: `Times New Roman`.
- Size: 小四, 12 pt.
- Alignment: justified.
- First-line indent: 2 Chinese characters.
- Line spacing: fixed 20 pt.
- Space before: 0 pt.
- Space after: 0 pt.

## Abstracts

Chinese abstract:

- Title `摘要`: `黑体`, 三号, centered, single line spacing, 24 pt before, 18 pt after.
- Abstract body: `宋体`, 小四 12 pt, justified, fixed 20 pt, 0 pt before/after.
- Keywords on same page after one blank line.
- `关键词：` itself should be bold.

English abstract:

- Title `ABSTRACT`: `Times New Roman`, 三号, bold, centered, single line spacing, 24 pt before, 18 pt after.
- Body: `Times New Roman`, 小四 12 pt, justified, fixed 20 pt, 0 pt before/after.
- Use English punctuation.
- `KEY WORDS:` itself should be bold.

## Table of Contents

- TOC title `目录`: `黑体`, 三号, centered, single line spacing, 24 pt before, 18 pt after.
- Chapter-level TOC entries: `黑体`, 小四 12 pt, fixed 20 pt, 6 pt before, 0 pt after.
- Other TOC entries: `宋体`, 小四 12 pt, fixed 20 pt, 0 pt before/after.
- Chapter entries left aligned.
- Level-1 section entries indent 1 Chinese character.
- Level-2 section entries indent 2 Chinese characters.
- Sequence number and title are separated by one Chinese character.
- Page number is right aligned.

## Headings

Chapter title:

- Example: `第1章  引言`.
- New page for each chapter.
- Chapter number uses Arabic numerals.
- Chapter number and title are separated by one Chinese character.
- Font: `黑体`, 三号, 16 pt.
- Alignment: centered.
- Line spacing: single.
- Space before: 24 pt.
- Space after: 18 pt.
- No punctuation at the end.
- The same style is used for `摘要`, `目录`, `参考文献`, `附录`, `致谢`, and other chapter-level parts listed in the TOC.

Level-1 section heading:

- Example: `2.1  实验方法`.
- Font: `黑体`, 四号, 14 pt.
- Alignment: left.
- Line spacing: fixed 20 pt.
- Space before: 24 pt.
- Space after: 6 pt.

Level-2 section heading:

- Example: `2.1.1  实验装置`.
- Font: `黑体`, 13 pt.
- Alignment: left.
- Line spacing: fixed 20 pt.
- Space before: 12 pt.
- Space after: 6 pt.

Level-3 section heading:

- Example: `2.1.1.1  实验结果`.
- Font: `黑体`, 小四, 12 pt.
- Alignment: left.
- Line spacing: fixed 20 pt.
- Space before: 12 pt.
- Space after: 6 pt.
- Generally avoid level-3 headings; do not use headings below level 3.

Heading QA:

- Keep headings with the following paragraph.
- Avoid page breaks between headings and their following text.
- Avoid a few isolated characters or a single final line at the end of a chapter.

## Figures

- Figure numbering: by chapter, e.g. `图2.1`.
- Figures must appear after first mention and not too far from that mention.
- Do not split a figure across pages.
- Figure text inside the image: 9-10.5 pt, legible.
- Figure caption is below the figure.
- Chinese caption: `宋体`, bold, 11 pt, centered, single line spacing, 6 pt before, 0 pt after.
- English caption, if any: `Times New Roman`, bold, 11 pt, centered, placed below the Chinese caption.
- Figure number and title are separated by one Chinese character.
- No punctuation at the end of figure title.
- Leave 10 pt blank spacing between figure and surrounding text; leave 20 pt between consecutive figures/tables if unavoidable.

## Tables

- Table numbering: by chapter, e.g. `表2.1`.
- Table cell text: `宋体`, 11 pt, single line spacing, 3 pt before, 3 pt after.
- Table caption is above the table.
- Chinese table caption: `宋体`, bold, 11 pt, centered, single line spacing, 6 pt before, 0 pt after.
- English table caption, if any: `Times New Roman`, bold, 11 pt, centered, below Chinese caption.
- Table number and title are separated by one Chinese character.
- No punctuation at the end of table title.
- Large tables may continue on following pages as `续表2.1  ...`; repeat the table header on continuation pages.
- Table notes: below table, `宋体` or `Times New Roman`, 11 pt, single line spacing, 6 pt before, 0 pt after.
- Leave 10 pt blank spacing between table and surrounding text; leave 20 pt between consecutive figures/tables if unavoidable.

## Equations

- Equations are numbered by chapter, e.g. `式（1.2）`.
- Equation body uses the same size as body text unless the expression requires otherwise.
- Place equation centered on a separate paragraph or with two Chinese-character indent; use one format consistently.
- Space before: 6 pt.
- Space after: 6 pt.
- Equation number appears at the right end of the line, with no connector line.

## References

- References are listed after the main body, not by chapter.
- Title `参考文献` uses the chapter title style.
- Reference body:
  - Chinese: `宋体`.
  - English: `Times New Roman`.
  - Size: 五号, 10.5 pt.
  - Fixed line spacing: 20 pt.
  - Space before/after: 0 pt.
  - Hanging indent: 2.5 Chinese characters.
- Use either sequential numbering or author-year consistently; do not mix them.
- For author-year references, list Chinese references before foreign-language references and sort author names alphabetically/lexicographically as appropriate.

## Verification Workflow

For USTC thesis-style output:

1. Generate DOCX using UTF-8 input, never shell pipelines for Chinese body text.
2. Apply USTC page, font, heading, table, figure, reference, header/footer, and page-number rules.
3. Validate DOCX XML for missing Chinese text, `�`, or suspicious `????`.
4. Export DOCX to PDF using Microsoft Word COM when available.
5. Render PDF to PNG with Poppler `pdftoppm`.
6. Visually inspect representative pages and all pages for final delivery:
   - cover/front matter, if present
   - abstract and TOC
   - first body chapter page
   - pages with headings, figures, tables, equations, references
7. Do not claim template compliance unless render-based QA has passed or the limitation is explicitly disclosed.
